Animetas is a generative collection of 10101 unique 1/1 avatars designed by @pixelGustavo and generated by @cyberh49.

The Animetas are virtual entities of humans (and other species) who minted their brains on the Ethereum blockchain, and live happily in the Animetaverse - a virtual world, inspired by the 80's and 90's pop culture, that was created at a time far in the future where earth became no longer habitable for humans.

The collection is very inclusive, and beside different species, it has a 1:1 female to male ratio, it has trans representation, characters of all ages, and a lot more surprises. Our variety of traits is unprecedented - 100+ types of clothes, 50+ types of hair styles, hats, masks, and more.

The collection drops on July 31st, 2021. The price of each piece will be 0.08 eth.

Roadmap

1. The first thing we're going to do after the initial drop on July 31st is start working on two airdrops, each of 10101 unique pieces - hovercars and weapons. As we have high standards, we estimate that each drop will take ~2-3 months.

2. We're also starting the creative process of the Anipetas project - the pets of the Animetaverse.

3. In parallel, we will start working on collabs with community members to make physical and metaverse merch and wearables.

4. After the airdrops, we will start working on retro arcade games like hovercars racing, classic fighting games, etc.

5. Our biggest dream is to make a comic and a TV series of Animetas. We don't know how or when it will happen, we just know we will make it happen.
