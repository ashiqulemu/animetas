SolBoredApes is a generative collection of 10,000 unique 1/1 avatars on the Solana network.

The SolBoredApes will be distribute fairly. Over 120+ attributes make each SolBoredApe unique.

The variety of traits span across different backgrounds, furs, hats, clothes eyes, mouths and earrings.

Apes belong on the Solana blockchain.
